3.3 magnitude quake in Christchurch

An earthquake struck near Lyttleton on Wednesday.

Christchurch residents have felt a 3.3 magnitude earthquake.

Geonet said the quake struck near Lyttleton about 12.26pm Wednesday.

The quake was 10 kilometres deep and the intensity was "light".

Residents felt the jolt across the city.

A post on the Rebuild Christchurch Facebook page read: "That was a sharp jolt".
