Trailer: 'The Art of Recovery' - Christchurch rebuild documentary

Christchurch film-maker Peter Young's new documentary, The Art of Recovery is set to premiere at the New Zealand International Film Festival next week. The film looks at how Cantabrians responded to the 2011 Christchurch earthquake with public murals, pop-up enterprises and inventive recreational options set up in the post-quake city.
